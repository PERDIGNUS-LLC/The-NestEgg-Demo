Meet the Founder

NestEgg was born from the belief that design should serve both beauty and life. Our founder — an engineer, artist, and father — sought to bridge human ingenuity with nature’s quiet wisdom.

What began as a personal project to create a safe, nurturing home for his son’s favorite backyard birds soon became a pursuit of ecological artistry — a movement toward harmony between craft and conservation.

Each NestEgg carries that purpose forward: precision where it matters, poetry where it breathes.

Principles that Shape Every Nest

Crafted with Purpose

From concept to casting, every curve and contour is intentional — designed for function, safety, and soul.

Inspired by Nature

We study avian behavior and environmental science to ensure each NestEgg harmonizes with the life it shelters.

Driven by Legacy

Every NestEgg is a promise — to our children and to theirs — that human hands can still build things that heal.

“Design is not invention — it is remembrance.”
