A Revolution in Conservation

The NestEgg is a direct response to the climate crisis. By rethinking materials and design, we've created a product that actively heals the environment.

Proprietary Necterra™ Material

Handmade with zero emissions from Necterra™, our proprietary blend of recycled, biodegradable fibers and natural binders.

Biomimetic Design

Based on university research, it mimics the thermal regulation, spatial dimensions, and protection of natural cavity nests for a safer, more effective home.

Modular System

A flexible design allows homeowners to mount it almost anywhere and support a wide variety of different bird species year after year.

The Silent Crisis

Your NestEgg is more than a product; it's a vital intervention. According to the 2022 State of the Birds report, North America has lost **3 billion birds** since 1970. This isn't a distant problem—it's a housing crisis happening in our own backyards.

A Home is Hard to Find

But habitat loss is only half the story. The scarcity of natural nesting sites creates intense competition where larger, more aggressive birds almost always drive smaller native species out of the few good cavities that remain.

This is where a crucial design consideration makes all the difference. The NestEgg features a Modular Entrance Hole system. Since cavity nesters are very particular about entrance size, using an opening that is simply too small for larger competitors transforms the NestEgg into a dedicated sanctuary. This crucial design consideration is a direct and strategic approach to creating ample, safe accommodation for the very species that need it most.
