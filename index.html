A Home is Hard to Find

But habitat loss is only half the story. The scarcity of natural nesting sites forces a critical choice. If a bird doesn't feel absolutely certain about the safety of its nest, it may refuse to reproduce, forgoing generational continuity.

This timeless predicament often ends in one of two ways: the nest falls prey to devastation by larger competitors, or the birds abandon it altogether. Both outcomes equate to population decline. The NestEgg™ is engineered to solve this dilemma, providing the security smaller birds need to thrive.
