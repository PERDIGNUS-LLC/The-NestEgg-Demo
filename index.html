A Revolution in Conservation

There's a deep irony in modern conservation: even the birdhouses meant to help are often part of the problem. Traditional wooden houses contribute to deforestation and emissions, a cycle that The NestEgg™ is designed to break. We believe helping birds shouldn't come at the expense of their habitat.

The Silent Crisis

Your NestEgg™ is more than a product; it's a vital intervention. According to the 2022 State of the Birds report, North America has lost **3 billion birds** since 1970. This isn't a distant problem‚Äîit's a housing crisis happening in our own backyards.

A Home is Hard to Find

But habitat loss is only half the story. The scarcity of natural nesting sites forces a critical choice. If a bird doesn't feel absolutely certain about the safety of its nest, it may refuse to reproduce, forgoing generational continuity.

This timeless predicament often ends in one of two ways: the nest falls prey to devastation by larger competitors, or the birds abandon it altogether. Both outcomes equate to population decline. The NestEgg™ is engineered to solve this dilemma, providing the security smaller birds need to thrive.
