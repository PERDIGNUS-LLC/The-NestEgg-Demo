The Birds We Build For

Every NestEgg is inspired by the species it serves. From the shape of the entrance to the depth of the chamber, design meets biology — ensuring every guest feels at home.

Eastern Bluebird

A symbol of renewal and joy, the bluebird thrives in open fields and quiet gardens — precisely the habitat NestEgg helps protect.

Carolina Wren

Bold and curious, this tiny engineer prefers covered perches and secure enclosures, making our rounded chambers its ideal refuge.

Tree Swallow

A swift aerial acrobat, the Tree Swallow’s nesting needs inspired our design for optimized ventilation and easy seasonal cleaning.
